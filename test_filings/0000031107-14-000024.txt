<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">October 22, 2014</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="center"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">THE EASTERN COMPANY REPORTS EARNINGS UP 35% FOR THE THIRD</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="center"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">QUARTER AND UP 13% FOR NINE MONTHS OF 2014</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Naugatuck, CT</font>&#8211;The Eastern Company (NASDAQ-EML)<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#160;</font>today announced the results of its operations for the third quarter and nine months of 2014.&#160;&#160;Net income for the third quarter was $2.4 million or $0.39 per diluted share compared to $1.8 million or $0.29 per diluted share that was reported in the third quarter of 2013, a 35% increase.&#160;&#160;Year to date earnings for the nine month period ended September 27, 2014 were $5.6 million or $0.90 per diluted share, compared to $5.0 million or $0.80 per diluted share for the same period in 2013, a 13% increase.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Net sales for the quarter were $35.8 million, compared to $34.3 million for the same period in 2013, a 5% increase.&#160;&#160;For the nine months of 2014 net sales were $106.4 million compared to $108.2 million in 2013, a 2% decrease.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: -1.8pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Leonard F. Leganza, Chairman, President and CEO stated, &#8220;The focus and emphasis we have continued to place on earnings and cash flow during the recent period of economic ups and downs and uncertainties have provided the Company with a solid financial foundation.&#160;&#160;We are confident that our current liquidity will be strong enough to sustain our product development projects which provide the continuing opportunity for organic growth in the markets we currently serve as well as to provide the liquidity we need to acquire businesses which will add long term growth opportunities for the Company.&#160;&#160;Those strategic objectives will not have any negative impact on our dividend policy and necessary capital expenditures.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: -1.8pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Leganza continued, &#8220;As the economy improves, we expect that our many diversified markets will also be favorably affected and will increase our current sales growth objectives.&#160;&#160;Among our ongoing projects, we are finalizing the establishment of a plant in North Carolina which will produce lightweight composite panels to supply sleeper cabs for a new line of Class 8 trucks and in addition will enable us to expand our marketing and sales efforts of composite products throughout the Eastern U.S.A.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: -1.8pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Leganza also stated, &#8220;At our Metal Products segment we have worked toward diversifying our operations toward expanding our contract casting efforts for products that will take us into markets beyond our proprietary mining products.&#160;&#160;In our Security Products segment we experienced, and were very pleased with, the increased market acceptance and sales of our &#8220;Flash Cash&#8221; systems for the commercial laundry market.&#160;&#160;The products and &#8220;Flash Cash&#8221; system has been well received and accepted in foreign markets.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Leganza concluded, &#8220;Our diversification, experienced management team and solid financial condition provide us with the assets we need to take advantage of growth opportunities when they present themselves as well as to provide organic growth.&#8221;</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block"><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: -1.8pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The Eastern Company is a 156-year-old manufacturer of industrial hardware, security products and metal castings.&#160;&#160;It operates from eleven locations in the U.S., Canada, Mexico, Taiwan and China. The diversity of the Company&#8217;s products helps it to respond to the changing requirements of a broad array of markets.</font></div>
